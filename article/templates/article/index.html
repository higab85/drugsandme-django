What is harm reduction?

Each year, thousands of drug-related deaths occur in the UK. It’s not easy to find safe and reliable drug info, leaving us vulnerable to overdosing, dangerous drug combinations, or uncomfortable experiences. Harm reduction is an approach that neither encourages, nor discourages drug use. Instead, it aims to lower the possible risks & harms of using drugs.
